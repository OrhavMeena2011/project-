About Our Project

Welcome to our comprehensive analysis of India's major state economies. This project provides detailed insights into the growth trajectories of five key states: Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh, focusing on their industrial and economic development from 2020 to 2025.

Our interactive visualization platform presents sector-wise analysis across Electronics, Automobile, Petroleum, and Agriculture industries. Through detailed graphs and data tables, we showcase:

  • Current growth rates and future projections
  • Sector-specific opportunities and challenges
  • Comparative analysis across different states
  • Economic indicators and development metrics

This data-driven approach helps stakeholders, investors, and policy makers understand the economic landscape and make informed decisions for future investments and policy formulation.
